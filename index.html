Priscy Hair & Beauty
Appointment Booking Automation System

A smart booking and staff assignment system built to eliminate manual scheduling for a luxury beauty brand. When a customer submits a booking form, the entire workflow — staff assignment, record creation, and workload rotation — happens automatically with zero manual input.
